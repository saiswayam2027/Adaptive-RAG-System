LEAVE AND REMOTE WORK POLICY — HR HANDBOOK, SECTION 2

2.1 Annual Leave
Full-time employees accrue 1.5 days of annual leave per completed month, capped
at 18 days per calendar year. Unused leave up to 5 days may be carried over to
the next calendar year; anything beyond that is forfeited unless local labor law
requires otherwise.

2.2 Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 paid sick days per year, no medical certificate
required for absences of 2 days or fewer. Absences of 3 or more consecutive days
require a medical certificate submitted within 5 working days of return.

2.3 Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week without prior approval.
Fully remote arrangements (more than 2 days per week) require manager approval
and must be renewed every 6 months. Engineers on the on-call rotation must be
reachable and have stable internet during their on-call shift regardless of
remote work arrangement.

2.4 Notice Periods
The standard notice period for resignation is 30 days for individual contributors
and 60 days for engineering managers and above. Notice periods may be reduced by
mutual agreement but require written sign-off from both the employee's manager
and HR.
