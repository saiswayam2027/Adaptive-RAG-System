VENDOR PROCUREMENT POLICY — FINANCE HANDBOOK, SECTION 6

6.1 Vendor Onboarding
Any new vendor contract above $5,000 annually requires a security
questionnaire reviewed by the Security team before signature, regardless of
whether the vendor will touch customer data. Vendors handling Restricted-tier
data additionally require a signed Data Processing Agreement per Legal's
Data Privacy Policy.

6.2 Purchase Order Approval
Purchase orders under $1,000 require manager approval only. Between $1,000
and $10,000 require Finance Business Partner approval. Above $10,000
requires both Finance Business Partner and department VP approval, tracked
in the procurement system separately from the expense report thresholds in
Section 3.1 of the Expense Policy, which apply only to individual employee
reimbursements, not vendor contracts.

6.3 Vendor Performance Review
Vendors with contracts above $25,000 annually are reviewed quarterly by the
requesting department alongside Finance. Underperforming vendors (missing
agreed SLAs twice in a review period) trigger a formal remediation plan;
three consecutive misses trigger contract review for non-renewal.

6.4 Sole-Source Justification
Procurement requests that bypass competitive bidding require a sole-source
justification memo approved by the department VP and Finance, explaining
why no comparable vendor was evaluated. This applies regardless of contract
value, unlike the value-based approval tiers in Section 6.2.
