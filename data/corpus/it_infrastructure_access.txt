IT INFRASTRUCTURE AND ACCESS MANAGEMENT — IT HANDBOOK, SECTION 1

1.1 Device Provisioning
New hires receive a company laptop pre-configured with endpoint security
software within 3 business days of their start date confirmation, separate
from the repository/production access process covered in the Engineering
onboarding handbook.

1.2 VPN and Network Access
Remote access to internal tools requires VPN connection with hardware-token
multi-factor authentication. VPN sessions time out after 12 hours of
inactivity and require re-authentication; this is distinct from the SSO
session timeout for web-based internal tools, which is 8 hours.

1.3 Software Procurement
Employees may self-serve install software from the approved catalog
without IT approval. Software not on the catalog requires a request through
the IT portal, reviewed within 5 business days, with Security review added
for any tool requesting access to customer data or source code.

1.4 Offboarding
All system access must be revoked within 4 hours of an employee's last day
for involuntary departures, and by end of the final business day for
voluntary departures. Payments and authentication service access (as
defined in the Engineering deployment policy) is revoked first, ahead of
general repository access, due to the elevated risk profile.

1.5 Password and Credential Policy
Passwords must be rotated every 180 days for standard accounts and every 90
days for accounts with Restricted-tier data access (see Legal's Data
Privacy Policy for the tier definition). Shared credentials are prohibited
except for designated service accounts, which are audited quarterly.
