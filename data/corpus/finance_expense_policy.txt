EXPENSE AND REIMBURSEMENT POLICY — FINANCE HANDBOOK, SECTION 3

3.1 Approval Thresholds
Expenses under $200 require only manager approval via the expense portal.
Expenses between $200 and $2,000 require approval from both the manager and
the department's Finance Business Partner. Expenses above $2,000 require
additional sign-off from the VP of Finance, and must include a business
justification memo attached to the expense report.

3.2 Travel Expense Rules
Airfare must be booked at least 14 days in advance except for approved
emergency travel, which requires Director-level approval logged in the
travel exceptions tracker. Economy class is standard for flights under 6
hours; business class requires VP approval regardless of flight duration.

3.3 Reimbursement Timeline
Approved expense reports are reimbursed within 10 business days of approval.
Reports submitted more than 60 days after the expense date are not eligible
for reimbursement except with CFO approval for documented extenuating
circumstances.

3.4 Corporate Card Misuse
Any personal use of a corporate card must be repaid within 5 business days
of discovery. Repeated misuse (more than 2 instances in a rolling 12-month
period) is escalated to HR and may result in card revocation regardless of
repayment status.

3.5 Vendor Payment Escalation
If a vendor invoice remains unpaid 30 days past terms, Accounts Payable
escalates to the requesting department's manager. If unresolved after 45
days, escalation goes to the Finance Business Partner, not the original
manager, to avoid conflicts of interest in vendor relationships.
