NEW ENGINEER ONBOARDING — ENGINEERING HANDBOOK, SECTION 1

1.1 Access Provisioning
New engineers receive read-only access to all internal repositories on day one.
Write access to a specific service's repository is granted only after the
engineer's manager approves it in the access request portal, which typically
takes 1-2 business days.

Access to the payments service repository additionally requires completing the
PCI compliance training module, which is assigned automatically on day one and
must be completed within the first two weeks.

1.2 Production Access
Direct production database access is not granted to engineers in their first 90
days. During this period, any production data investigation must be done through
a senior engineer or via the read-only analytics replica.

1.3 On-Call Rotation
Engineers join the on-call rotation only after completing 90 days of employment
and shadowing at least two on-call shifts with a senior engineer. The on-call
rotation covers Sev-1 and Sev-2 incidents as defined in the Security Incident
Response section.

1.4 Required Training
All new engineers must complete: (1) the security awareness training, (2) the
incident response tabletop exercise, and (3) the deployment policy walkthrough,
all within the first 30 days. Engineers working on the payments or authentication
services have two additional required modules covering PCI-DSS and OAuth token
handling respectively.
