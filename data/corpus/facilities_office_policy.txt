FACILITIES AND OFFICE POLICY — FACILITIES HANDBOOK, SECTION 1

1.1 Desk and Room Booking
Hot desks are booked via the office app up to 2 weeks in advance. Meeting
rooms with capacity over 8 people require booking at least 24 hours ahead
during standard business hours; smaller rooms can be booked with no advance
notice if shown available.

1.2 Guest Access
Visitors must be registered at least 2 hours before arrival and escorted at
all times in Restricted-access areas, which include the server room and any
floor housing the Payments or Security teams. Visitors are never granted
network credentials, only guest wifi.

1.3 Equipment Requests
Standard equipment (monitor, keyboard, chair) requests are fulfilled within
5 business days. Ergonomic assessments and specialized equipment requests
require a form submitted with manager approval and are fulfilled within 15
business days.

1.4 Office Closure and Weather Policy
The office closes if local authorities issue a severe weather advisory.
Employees are notified via the emergency SMS system, which is separate from
the on-call paging system used for Sev-1/Sev-2 incidents described in the
Security Incident Response policy.

1.5 Badge Access Revocation
Physical badge access is revoked on the same schedule as system access
during offboarding: within 4 hours for involuntary departures and by end of
day for voluntary departures, coordinated between Facilities and IT so
neither team acts without the other's confirmation.
